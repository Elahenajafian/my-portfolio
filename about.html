About Elahe

Front End Developer from Quchan, Iran

Elahe was born and raised in a part of the world called Iran. She loved animals ever since she was a young child. She had some animals throughout her childhood, like cats, chickens, roosters, ducks, and once a turtle. They were her friends. Friends that would always remain true to her. She was always concerned about them and did everything she could to make sure they were okay. In fact, her dad used to call her "The protector of animal rights". She took pride in that title.
In Iran, street animals usually are not welcome into people's lives and houses, for good reasons. We are concerned about our health issues since the animals spend a lot time in the rubbish looking for food. Among street animals, dogs are misunderstood the most. We are usually scared of them and have no experience of how friendly and loving they could be with humans. Elahe's childhood dream was to build an animal shelter;
somewhere that street animals can be hugged and kissed, feel warm, and and enjoy a nice meal. She wants to educate children about animals and encourage them to approach them with kindness, so that animals and humans both can experience the wonderful experience of each other's company.
