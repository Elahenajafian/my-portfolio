About Elahe

Elahe was born in a part of the world called Iran. She loved animals since she was a young child. She had some animals through her childhood like cats, chickens, roosters, ducks, and a turtle. They were like her friends that would always remain true to her. She was always concerned about them and did everything she could to make sure they were okay. In fact, her dad used to call her "The protector of animal rights".
Elahe's childhood dream is to build an animal shelter; somewhere that street animals can find love, shelter, and food. In Iran, street animals are not treated very well in general. Dogs in particular, are considered scary and unfriendly. Elahe wants to take care of dogs, and famaliarize people with these beautiful animals, and how we have been misunderstood about them, and how we don't have to deprive ourselves of their wonderful company.
