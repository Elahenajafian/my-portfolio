Elahe Najafian

Full-Stack Developer living in Sydney, Australia

I was born and raised in Iran, where I studied English Translation for my bachelor's degree and English Teaching for my master's. During this time, I met and married the love of my life, Jesse. We lived together in Iran for five years, and it was during this period that I discovered my passion for coding.
My journey into the world of programming began with SheCodes workshops. These workshops were transformative, building my confidence and igniting my love for coding. They empowered me to explore further resources and dive into self-learning. Copilot, Microsoft’s AI, became an invaluable mentor in my learning process—guiding me every step of the way.
Later, I discovered the Mimo application, which significantly helped me to solidify my understanding of coding concepts and the logic behind different programming languages. I absolutely love learning the logic behind coding languages and solving complex problems through code. This journey has not only been challenging but incredibly rewarding, and I am eager to continue learning and growing in this field.
The blend of structured learning through SheCodes, supportive mentorship from Copilot, and practical exercises on Mimo has been instrumental in my journey to becoming a proficient coder. I look forward to expanding my knowledge and skills even further.
